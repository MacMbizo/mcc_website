Welcome to Midlands Christian College

Midlands Christian College (MCC) is a private, internationally accredited, co-educational Christian high school located in the Midlands province of Zimbabwe. A member of the ATS-CHISZ Schools group and the Association of Christian Schools International, this co-educational school has been offering quality education to pupils since 1987.

About Midlands Christian College

Midlands Christian College was founded in 1987 to offer quality education to the Gweru community. Located in the Midlands province of Zimbabwe, MCC has grown to become one of the leading educational institutions in the country.

As a private co-educational high school with approximately 450 students from Form 1 to Form 6, we provide both boarding facilities and day-scholar options. Our campus is situated in New Christmas Gift, Gweru, Zimbabwe.

We are proud to be a member of the Association of Trust Schools (ATS) and the Association of Christian Schools International (ACSI), which ensures we maintain high standards in both academics and character development.

Academic Excellence

Our Curriculum

Midlands Christian College provides a broad and balanced curriculum designed to meet the needs of our pupils with varying abilities. Within this framework, one of our Expected Outcomes is, with Christ as the centre of truth, to develop pupils who have qualifications in English, Mathematics, Science and Computing.

Our academic program is overseen by the Vice Principal and Director of Academics, working with a well-qualified team of Subject Managers who manage the day-to-day running of their subjects.

Examination Boards

Pupils can sit for examinations under the following examination boards:

  • Cambridge
  • ZIMSEC
  • DELF DALF
  • HSK (Chinese)
  • London College of Music

Additionally, all pupils sit for the International Computer Driving Licence (ICDL) under the Zimbabwe Computer Society.

Academic Offerings

1 Subjects Offered at Junior Level

English Language, Shona, French, Ndebele, Mandarin, Combined Science, Religious Studies, Geography, History, Mathematics, Commerce, Music, Physical Education

Pupils are required to choose one technical option subject from: Art and Design; Agriculture; Design and Technology (Graphic Products, or Resistant Materials) or Fashion and Textiles.

2 Subjects Offered at IGCSE Level

English as a First Language; English Literature; Core Mathematics; Extended Mathematics; Additional Mathematics, Combined Science; Physical Science; Core Biology; Extended Biology; Chemistry; Physics; Geography; History; Accounting; Commerce; Religious Studies; Physical Education; French; Chinese and Shona.

Pupils continue with the one technical option subject which they started at the junior level.

3 Subjects Offered at AS Level and A Level

Mathematics; Chemistry; Biology; Physics; History; Geography; Divinity; English Literature; English Language; Art and Design; Fashion and Design; Design and Technology; Business Studies; Accounting; Economics; Computer Science; Chinese; French; Travel and Tourism.

Boarding Life

A Home Away From Home

Midlands Christian College offers both day school and boarding places, with boarders forming the majority of our intake. Our boarding facilities provide a supportive, structured environment that balances academic focus with personal development.

Located on campus, Eagle House, Kestrel House and Oasis offer full boarding to pupils from Form 1 to Upper 6. Students are accommodated either in dormitory-style or shared room accommodation, supervised by full-time hostel supervisors and duty staff who care for the pupils' needs.

Our hostels are run by Christian staff who uphold the rules by which the College is run and teach Christian principles. These hostels have structured programmes which ensure adequate study time, spiritual input, a reasonable bed time, and time to relax.

Our Christian Foundation

Support Systems

Shadow Groups

Based on the principle found in Acts 5:15-16, every pupil in the College selects a member of staff whose shadow they will fall under – boys choosing a male member of staff and girls choosing a female member of staff. These resultant groups bond and become a close-knit support for each other through the ups and downs of life as they also grow together in the ways of the Lord.

Hostel Church

Boarders attend Hostel Church in the school hall on Sunday mornings. There is a time of Praise and Worship as well as prayer. Guest Pastors and speakers are invited to come and share with our pupils and dovetails with our Biblical Studies programme.
